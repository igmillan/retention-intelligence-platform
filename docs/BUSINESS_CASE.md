# Business Case — User Retention & Churn Prediction

---

## Executive Overview

Subscription retention is one of the most critical revenue levers for digital
platform ecosystems such as Apple Music and Apple TV+.

Proactively identifying users likely to churn before cancellation enables
retention teams to intervene strategically, preserve recurring revenue, and
improve long-term customer lifetime value.

This project develops a predictive retention intelligence framework that
identifies churn risk, explains behavioral deterioration drivers, and
prioritizes intervention opportunities across the user base.

---

## Strategic Problem

Traditional churn management approaches are often reactive, relying on lagging
indicators after churn has already occurred.

This limits business effectiveness by:

- delaying intervention timing,
- reducing personalization quality,
- misallocating retention spend,
- lowering retention ROI.

---

## Solution Delivered

The solution provides:

- 30-day churn risk prediction,
- behavioral explainability via SHAP,
- revenue-at-risk prioritization,
- tailored retention playbook recommendation.

---

## Key Strategic Findings

### Behavioral deterioration is the strongest churn indicator.
Declining watch/listen activity materially outperforms demographic factors.

### Inactivity sharply increases churn probability.
Recency degradation is a critical early-warning signal.

### User value concentration matters.
A minority of critical-risk users drives disproportionate revenue exposure.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

### Focus retention efforts on high-risk/high-value cohorts.
### Tailor intervention by behavioral deterioration type.
### Use engagement decay metrics as leading churn signals.

---

## Expected Business Impact

A retention intelligence system of this nature can improve:

- proactive churn mitigation,
- campaign personalization,
- retention ROI,
- resource prioritization effectiveness.
